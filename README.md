## 👩🏻‍💻 About me
🧑🏻 My name is Jocimar Borges Júnior, or just "Júnior" (common call).  
🎓 I'm a Student of Bachelor's in Software Engineering at UTFPR-CP (Federal Technological University of Paraná).  
📆 I was born in 2005.  
☕ And I'm absolutely in love with coffee and Japanese culture. That's awesome!

My first experience with coding was back when everyone had a Wix website. I remember trying found ways to change the elements, customize them more and make the text stand out. I used to spend hours tweaking it and and wouldn’t even notice the time passing. The thrill of being able to structure pages on my own was amazing.  

It’s like a puzzle: you start piece by piece, no one understands what’s being formed, at first it’s ugly and strange… and then, done! You end up with a work of art made of code.
